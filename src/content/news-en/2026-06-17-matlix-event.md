**VDA 231-301 is becoming a key enabler for real-world material data exchange across industry.**

On June 17, 2026, Matplus GmbH will host a workshop in Wuppertal focusing on the exchange of material-related information across value chains – without media discontinuities.


The objective is to support companies in exchanging material data efficiently and in an agile manner – based on the open standard VDA 231-301.

The workshop is aimed at decision-makers, end users, and IT professionals who want to integrate material information into existing system landscapes such as ERP and PLM systems while avoiding unnecessary overhead. Concepts from data spaces such as DCP, DAPS, or ODRL can be integrated where required.
