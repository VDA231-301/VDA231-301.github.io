**VDA 231-301 is becoming a key enabler for real-world material data exchange across industry.**

On June 17, 2026, Matplus GmbH will host a workshop in Wuppertal focusing on the exchange of material-related information across value chains – without media discontinuities.

The workshop will present the new service offering MatliX, developed jointly with Litexa GmbH. Its goal is to support companies in efficiently and flexibly exchanging material data based on the open standard VDA 231-301.


The event is aimed at decision-makers, end users, and IT professionals who want to integrate material data into existing system landscapes such as ERP and PLM while avoiding unnecessary overhead.

MatliX follows a pragmatic approach while remaining future-ready:  
Concepts from data spaces such as DCP, DAPS, or ODRL can be integrated if needed. In addition, MatliX aligns with IDS RAM and Gaia-X principles to ensure interoperability with European data ecosystems.

The workshop is limited to 20 participants and provides a practical platform for exchange and discussion on the digital use of material-related data.
